## Biography Summary

### Early Life

Red Cloud was born in 1822 near the Platte River in present-day Nebraska, into the Oglala Lakota tribe. He grew up during a period of increasing pressure from U.S. expansion into traditional Lakota territories. He became a respected warrior and leader, known for his strategic thinking and diplomatic skills.

### Career/Public Life

Red Cloud is best known for leading Red Cloud's War (1866-1868), a successful military campaign against the U.S. Army that forced the abandonment of forts along the Bozeman Trail. This was one of the most successful Native American military campaigns against U.S. forces. After the war, Red Cloud became a prominent diplomat, negotiating treaties and advocating for Lakota rights. He traveled extensively, including to Washington, D.C., to meet with U.S. officials.
